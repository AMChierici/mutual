# Code of Conduct

We're building software about people taking care of each other. Behave accordingly.

- Be kind. Assume good faith.
- Disagree with ideas, not with people.
- No harassment, no discrimination, no surveillance.
- If a maintainer asks you to stop, stop. Appeals can be made privately.

Violations: a warning, then a temporary ban, then a permanent ban. Maintainers' judgment is final, and we will explain our reasoning.

Reports: open a private issue or email maintainers@_TBD_.
