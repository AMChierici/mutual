# Freelancer Collective Pool Policy Template

For a defined group of freelancers (writers, devs, designers) who already know each other and want a buffer against income gaps and equipment failures.

> **Important:** in many jurisdictions, opening this kind of pool to strangers crosses into regulated insurance territory. Keep membership to people you actually know or who join via clear affinity (same co-working space, same union, same Discord that's been around for years).

## Pool

- **Name:** _Your collective_
- **Members:** Cap at 25. Membership requires endorsement from 2 existing members.
- **Currency:** _your local_

## Contributions

- Flat: 2% of gross monthly income, self-reported
- Floor: $50/month
- Ceiling: $400/month (so high earners aren't carrying the pool unfairly)
- Annual true-up against tax filings (honor system + spot checks)

## What's covered

- Equipment failure: laptop, camera, instruments — up to $1500/incident
- Income gap: 50% of declared average monthly income for up to 2 months, after a 30-day waiting period since the last paid gig
- Mental health: up to $500/year toward therapy or coaching
- Conference travel that fell through (non-refundable bookings, up to $400)

## What's not covered

- Medical (use real insurance + your country's safety net)
- Voluntary career breaks
- Income gaps where you turned down available work
- Anything happening in your first 60 days of membership (waiting period)

## Limits

- Per-member annual max: 4x your average monthly contribution
- Pool minimum balance: 3 months of expected payouts. Below that, only equipment claims pay.

## Governance

- **Equipment claims with receipts:** jury of 3 randomly selected members, simple majority
- **Income gap claims:** jury of 5, must show evidence of job-seeking, simple majority
- **Mental health claims:** auto-approved on receipt, no questions, capped at $500/year
- **Disputes:** full-pool vote, 2/3 supermajority required to overturn jury

## Premium review

Quarterly. Open Mutual's reserving model output, review with the pool, adjust together.

## Conflict of interest

Members do not vote on their own claims. Jurors who have an active claim are excluded from juries until theirs is resolved.

## Pool retirement

12 months notice. Remaining balance split proportional to lifetime contributions.
