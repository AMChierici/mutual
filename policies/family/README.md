# Family Pool Policy Template

A starter policy for a household pool (parents + kids). Fork it, edit it, commit your version into your own repo or keep it private.

## Pool

- **Name:** _Your family name_
- **Members:** All household members aged 5+
- **Currency:** USD (change as needed)
- **Holding account:** Joint savings at _your bank_, account ending _XXXX_

## Contributions

- Adults: $100/month each
- Kids age 12+: $10/month from allowance
- Kids under 12: $2/month, symbolic
- Contributions due 1st of the month, logged in Mutual

## What's covered

- Lost or broken phones, laptops, tablets, AirPods, school instruments
- Bike repair or replacement
- Lost retainers, glasses
- Co-pays and deductibles not covered by real insurance
- Emergency vet bills up to $500/incident
- Travel: lost luggage, missed flight rebooking up to $300/incident
- "Stupid tax" — one parent-approved discretionary payout per kid per year

## What's not covered

- Anything covered by real insurance (file there first)
- Intentional damage
- Anything over $1000/incident (use savings or real insurance)
- Wants, not needs (a new game console because the old one is "boring")

## Limits

- Per-incident max: $500 (with parent override up to $1000)
- Per-member annual max: $1500
- Pool maintains minimum balance of $500 — claims pause if pool drops below

## Governance

- **Claims under $50:** auto-approved, logged for transparency
- **Claims $50-$200:** majority vote of household members aged 12+
- **Claims $200+:** unanimous vote of adults, with kids advisory
- **Disputed claims:** family meeting, decision logged in Mutual
- **Parent veto:** either parent can veto any claim, must give written reason in the app

## Premium review

Every 6 months: look at pool balance, claim history, and pricing model output. Adjust contributions if needed. Don't change rules mid-claim.

- _Add cases here as you encounter them. This is the most valuable section over time._

## Pool retirement

If the pool dissolves, remaining balance is split equally across members who contributed for at least 6 months.
